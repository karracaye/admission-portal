Below are the requirements needed to be accomplished and submitted

FOR INCOMING FIRST YEAR STUDENTS

FRESHMEN

- Report Card/ Form 138

- Certificate of Good Moral Character

- Authenticated PSA Birth Certificate

- Authenticated PSA Marriage Certificate (Married student only.)

- 2x2 colored picture (recent picture and a blue background)

- Medical Examination Certificate (CSPC Medical Clinic)

- CSPC-College Admission Test Result

- National Career Assessment Examnination Result (NCAE)

FOR TRANSFEREE STUDENTS

IRREGULAR/FRESHMEN

- Certificate of Transfer Credentials/Honorable Dismissal

- Certified Copy of Grades/Official Transcript of Records (OTR)

- Certificate of Good Moral Character

- Authenticated PSA Birth Certificate

- Authenticated PSA Marriage Certificate (Married student only.)

- 2x2 colored picture (recent picture and a blue background)

- Medical Examination Certificate (CSPC Medical Clinic)

- CSPC-College Admission Test Result

- National Career Assessment Examnination Result (NCAE)
